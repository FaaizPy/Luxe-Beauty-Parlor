Meet Your Dentist
Dr. Abdul Ghani
BDS, FCPS (Orthodontics) · Chief Dental Surgeon

Dr. Abdul Ghani is a Fellow of the College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan with a specialization in Orthodontics and over 16 years of dedicated clinical practice in Rawalpindi.

After completing his BDS with distinction, Dr. Ghani pursued advanced training in cosmetic dentistry and implantology at the Aga Khan University Hospital and completed his FCPS residency at PMDC-accredited institutions across Pakistan.

His clinic philosophy is simple: treat every patient the way you would want your own family treated. Every procedure is explained clearly, every option is presented honestly, and every treatment plan is tailored to the individual — never a one-size-fits-all approach.

"My goal isn't just to fix teeth — it's to restore confidence. When a patient smiles as they leave, that's when I know we've done our job right."

BDS — King Edward Medical FCPS (Orthodontics) PMDC Registered Implantology Certified Pain-Free Specialist
