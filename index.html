Our Story

A Studio Built for
Your Glow

Luxe Beauty Studio was born from a single belief: every woman deserves to feel extraordinary. Founded in 2018 by Sara Noor, a certified makeup artist trained in Dubai and London, our studio has grown into Islamabad's most trusted name in luxury beauty.

From intimate bridal sessions to professional skincare treatments, every service is delivered with obsessive attention to detail, the finest international products, and a genuine love for celebrating your unique beauty.
